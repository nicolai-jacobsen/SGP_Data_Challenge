
Boat Logs Columns:
1. BOAT: The unique identifier for the boat.
2. DATETIME: The date and time of the log entry.
3. TIME_LOCAL_unk: Local time of the log entry.
4. LATITUDE_GPS_unk: The latitude coordinate from the GPS.
5. LONGITUDE_GPS_unk: The longitude coordinate from the GPS.
6. BOAT_SPEED_km_h_1: The speed of the boat in kilometers per hour.
7. GPS_SOG_km_h_1: Speed over ground from GPS in kilometers per hour.
8. HEADING_deg: The heading direction of the boat in degrees.
9. TWA_SGP_deg: True wind angle in degrees.
10. TWS_SGP_km_h_1: True wind speed in kilometers per hour.
11. TWD_SGP_deg: True wind direction in degrees.
12. AWA_SGP_deg: Apparent wind angle in degrees.
13. RATE_YAW_deg_s_1: Yaw rate or rate of turn in degrees per second.
14. GPS_COG_deg: Course over ground from GPS in degrees.
15. LEEWAY_deg: Leeway angle in degrees.
16. VMG_km_h_1: Velocity made good in kilometers per hour. (Not reliable).
17. ANGLE_CA1_deg: Wing Camber 1 (bottom) in degrees.
18. ANGLE_CA2_deg: Wing Camber 2 (middle) in degrees.
19. ANGLE_CA3_deg: Wing Camber 3 (middle) in degrees.
20. ANGLE_CA4_deg: Wing Camber 4 (middle) in degrees.
21. ANGLE_CA5_deg: Wing Camber 5 (middle) in degrees.
22. ANGLE_CA6_deg: Wing Camber 6 (top) in degrees.
23. ANGLE_WING_TWIST_deg: Angle of wing twist in degrees (difference between CA1 and CA6).
24. ANGLE_WING_ROT_deg: Wing rotation, of the first element in degrees.
25. PITCH_deg: The pitch angle of the boat in degrees.
26. HEEL_deg: The heel angle of the boat in degrees.
27. LENGTH_RH_P_mm: Port hull ride height in millimeters.
28. LENGTH_RH_S_mm: Starboard hull ride height in millimeters.
29. LENGTH_RH_BOW_mm: Bow ride height in millimeters.
30. ANGLE_RUDDER_deg: Angle of the rudder in degrees.
31. ANGLE_RUD_AVG_deg: Average lift angle of the rudder.
32. ANGLE_RUD_DIFF_TACK_deg: Tack corrected rudder differential angle in degrees.
33. ANGLE_DB_RAKE_P_deg: Angle of foil rake port side in degrees.
34. ANGLE_DB_RAKE_S_deg: Angle of foil rake starboard side in degrees.
35. ANGLE_DB_CANT_P_deg: Angle of foil cant port side in degrees.
36. ANGLE_DB_CANT_S_deg: Angle of foil cant starboard side in degrees.
37. TRK_LEG_NUM_TOT_unk: Total number of legs of the race.
38. TRK_LEG_NUM_unk: Current leg number.
39. TRK_RACE_NUM_unk: Current race number.
40. PC_TTS_s: Time to start in seconds (countdown).
41. PC_TTK_s: Time to kill in seconds.
42. PC_TTB_s: Time to boundary in seconds.
43. PC_DTB_m: Distance to boundary in meters.
44. PC_DTL_m: Distance to start line in meters.
45. PC_TTM_s: Time to manoeuvre in seconds.
